Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

Dough:
1 cup warm water
2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
1 teaspoon sugar
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons olive oil
Sauce:
1 (15-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1/2 teaspoon dried basil
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1-In a small bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Let it sit until foamy.
2-In a larger bowl, combine flour, salt, and any desired herbs. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
3-Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
4-Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise in a warm place until doubled in size.
5-To make sauce, simmer crushed tomatoes, garlic, oregano, basil, and other desired seasonings until thickened.
6-Chop your favorite toppings: vegetables, meats, cheeses, etc.
7-Preheat your oven to a high temperature (450-500°F or 230-260°C). If you have a pizza stone, preheat it in the oven.
8-On a floured surface, stretch or roll out the dough to your desired thickness.
9-place the dough onto a pizza pan, or pizza stone.
10-Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
11-Sprinkle cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings.
12-Bake the pizza until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
13-Let the pizza cool slightly before slicing and serving.
